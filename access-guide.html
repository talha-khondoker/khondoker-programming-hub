How to Get Access to Classes

Steps to Access Classes

Once you have successfully enrolled in the course, follow these steps to get access to the online classes:

  1. Check your email for an access link or class schedule. This will be sent to the email address you provided after registration.
  2. Ensure you have the necessary software installed (e.g., Zoom, Google Classroom).
  3. The recorded classes will be uploaded to Google Drive from where you can access the classes.
  4. If you do not understand anything, there will be support sessions every day and there will always be support in the group (WhatsApp & Discord).
  5. Problems will be practiced from various online Judges including Codeforces, Codechef, and Leetcode.
  6. Click on the provided link and join the class at the scheduled time.
  7. Keep your payment receipt and sign-up confirmation handy in case verification is required.
